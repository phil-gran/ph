How to Reschedule

  1. Open students accout/profile in CRM
  2. Change Status to Rescheduling
  3. Check Time Converter
  4. Update CRM time and datein IST.
  5. Place your notes as detailed as possible
  6. Click Submit
  7. Post resched format in Slack

Posting of rescheduling on slack:

 Student's name (hyperlink this one ) + Number + Date (Monday-Friday) + Time (in IST or student's time zone) + Tag your respective TLs.

 Your TLs will check your rescheduling. Please make sure you convert the time in IST correctly. Leave a note in the CRM of the rescheduled date and time.

 NO RESCHEDULING AT 6:00 IST and beyond.

 Reschedule only every 21:30 IST - 5:30 IST.

 Reschedule within the next 3 days = the longer the delay, the higher chance that students' motivation will lessen. Try to make them understand that out of 700 bookings, your application has been considered, so 15-20 minutes.

Do not ask if students are available, do everything to make them take it now or later.
